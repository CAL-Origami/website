East Bay Origami Convention 2016

The East Bay Origami Convention is an origami convention held at UC Berkeley. We are currently doing our best to create a low-cost, amazing event for the many origami enthusiasts in the area.

This will be the first year since 2013 that the convention will be held. For that reason, we have chosen the theme Rebirth and Renewal for the event. We look forward to seeing you here!

SUBMITTING A MODEL (for display)
If you're interested in submitting a model, models do not have to be original. As long as proper documentation is provided, such as the name and author of the model, and the folder's name, you may bring any model to the convention to display.

It does not have to be part of the Theme.

CONVENTION BOOK
We are going to have a Convention Book this year! We compiled diagrams and crease patterns from many contributors to create a fantastic convention book this year. Although they will not arrive in time, they will be available for purchase at the convention and we will be shipping them out afterwards. If you would like to order a book but are not attending the convention, email us at eastbayorigamicon@gmail.com.
